The Philosophy

Why Kintsugi?

Named after the Japanese art of repairing broken pottery with gold, our name signifies our belief that systems are strengthened through strategic refinement and resilience.

Our founder, Priya Vinitha Rajan, is an Electronics and Communication Engineer who honed her expertise within the high-complexity environments of Amdocs and IBS. Having managed mission-critical billing and CRM systems for global leaders, she founded Kintsugi to provide SMEs with the same "enterprise-grade" precision.

We operate as a remote-first studio based in Trivandrum, working with a hand-picked network of specialists to deliver high-impact results without agency overhead.
